About the Temple

Sri Kalarickal Devi Temple is a centuries-old temple following traditional Kerala temple architecture and rituals. The temple is dedicated to Sri Raktheswari Moorthi, Lord Shiva and Parvathi are known for its spiritual serenity.

  • Presiding Deity: Sri Raktheswari Moorthi
  • Architecture: Kerala Traditional Style
  • Main Festival: Annual Utsavam
